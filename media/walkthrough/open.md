## Open your first canvas

Click **Open canvas** on the left, or run **Braid: Open** from the Command Palette (`Ctrl/Cmd+Shift+P`) anytime.

You can also manage multiple canvases (new / rename / delete) from the **Braid** icon in the Activity Bar.

> Empty canvas? **Double-click** or **right-click** empty space to create a new conversation root node.
